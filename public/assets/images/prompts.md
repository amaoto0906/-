# Final Image Prompt Set

All images were generated with the built-in image generation tool as realistic, fictional, logo-free assets for a marine aquaculture operations app. Shared constraints: no readable text, no logos, no watermark, no real-person likeness requirement, safe professional aquaculture context, plausible equipment, and clean UI-friendly composition.

## Added Analytics Asset

- `forecast-analytics`: Aquaculture manager reviewing unreadable forecast charts on a rugged tablet near offshore sea cages, calm field analytics mood.

## Existing Primary Assets

- `hero-login`: Fictional Japanese aquaculture worker with visible face checking a rugged tablet beside offshore sea cages.
- `dashboard-aerial`: Elevated aerial view of a Japanese coastal bay with circular offshore fish cages.
- `water-quality`: Worker handling a water quality probe and handheld meter on a marine cage walkway.
- `feeding-management`: Worker monitoring feed equipment near an offshore cage.
- `fish-health`: Healthy yellowtail and sea bream-like fish swimming underwater inside a subtle net cage.
- `alert-monitoring`: Moderate waves and overcast sky around offshore cages, worker calmly checking an unreadable tablet alert.
- `empty-no-alerts`: Product-like photo cutout of an inactive safety beacon, sensor buoy, and rope.
